About CAGAD

The Centre for Advancement of Governance and Development (CAGAD) is an independent, non-governmental, non-profit, non-partisan, and research-centered think tank focused on advancing good governance, accountability, security, and development in Nigeria and Africa through evidence-based research, policy analysis, advocacy for transparency and accountability in governance, policy advisory, and civic and stakeholder engagement.

We seek to promote bold ideas and inspire sustainable solutions that will address Nigeria and Africa's most pressing governance, security and development challenges.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become Nigeria and Africa's leading think tank, driving impactful change in advancing good governance, accountability, improved security, and sustainable development through research, policy dialogue, capacity-building, civic education and awareness, technological innovation, and multi-stakeholder engagement.

Our Mission

CAGAD's mission is to promote responsive governance, strengthen public leadership accountability, foster peacebuilding, and improve human security and inclusive development through research, civic and stakeholder engagements, capacity building, and policy advocacy and advisory services.

We do this by expanding citizens' and stakeholders' understanding of best governance practices and the impacts of policies on development and providing long-term solutions to challenges through cutting-edge policy research, policy advisory, advocacy, roundtables, and nonpartisan engagement and dialogue.

Our Values

Excellence

We strive for the highest quality in all our research, analysis, and recommendations.

Nonpartisanship

We maintain impartiality and do not align with any political party or ideology.

Integrity

We uphold the highest ethical standards in all our activities and relationships.

Inclusivity

We ensure diverse voices and perspectives are represented in our work.

Transparency

We are open about our methods, funding, and decision-making processes.

Objectivity

We base our findings and recommendations on evidence and rigorous analysis.

Our values are encapsulated in the acronym: ENIITO

CAGAD Pledge

"I commit myself to furthering the cause of justice, equity, fairness, accountability, and good governance in my country. I stand courageously to identify and question anyone or group engaging in behaviour that contributes to inequity, injustice, prejudice, and impunity, including abuse of power, privileges, unaccountability, and misgovernance. I also accept the constructive and civil critique of others to ensure I maintain my commitment to equity, fairness, and collective good above personal good, despite temptations to do otherwise. I pledge myself to the service of others, irrespective of their background, and do all in my power to be a good leader in advancing better governance, accountability, and development in my country, Africa, and the world. So help me God."
