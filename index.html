About CAGAD

The Centre for Advancement of Governance and Development (CAGAD) is an independent, non-profit, non-governmental, non-partisan, and research-centred think tank focused on advancing governance, accountability, security, and sustainable development in Nigeria and Africa.

We deliver evidence-based research, policy analysis, civic education, advocacy and policy advisory to tackle pressing governance and development challenges. Through bold ideas, cutting-edge research, digital innovation, inclusive dialogue, and stakeholder engagement, we empower citizens, strengthen ethical public leadership, and drive institutional transparency and credibility, especially in fragile or transitional contexts.

CAGAD connects research to action, offering foresighted, practical policy solutions, analysis, and recommendations that advance democracy, peacebuilding, accountability, and human security, while aligning our work and recommendations with global and regional best practices.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become Nigeria and Africa's leading think tank, driving impactful change in advancing good governance, accountability, improved security, and sustainable development through research, policy dialogue, capacity-building, civic education and awareness, technological innovation, and multi-stakeholder engagement.

Our Mission

CAGAD's mission is to promote responsive governance, strengthen public leadership accountability, foster citizens' participation in governance, improve human security and sustainable development through evidence-based research, civic and stakeholder engagements, policy advocacy and advisory.

CAGAD Pledge

“I commit myself to furthering the cause of good governance, justice, equity, and accountability in my country. I stand courageously to identify and question anyone or group engaging in behaviour that contributes to injustice, inequity, and underdevelopment, including abuse of power, privileges, impunity, and misgovernance. I also accept the constructive and civil critique of others to ensure I maintain my commitment to fairness and collective good above personal objectives, despite temptations to do otherwise. I pledge myself to the service of others, irrespective of their background, and do all in my power to be a good leader in advancing good governance, accountability, and development in my country, Africa, and the world. So help me God.”

Our Programmes

Elections Credibility Monitoring

Monitoring the credibility of elections in Nigeria and Africa, and providing independent, evidence-based, and non-partisan assessments of elections and electoral processes at regional, national and subnational levels.

Democracy Monitoring Programme

Tracking the health of democracy in Nigeria and Africa by monitoring trends in constitutional governance, the rule of law, civic freedom, civil liberties, democratic institutions, and citizens' participation in governance.

Accountability Monitoring Programme

Monitoring and tracking government performance, transparent budgeting, fiscal responsibility, service delivery on promises, anti-corruption initiatives and evidence for abuse of office, public trust and institutional credibility.

Security Monitoring Programme

Monitoring and tracking security incidents, analysing conflict and insecurity trends, including terrorism, violent extremism, organised crime, and communal violence, providing early warning, conflict mapping, policy briefs, and advisory guides for governments, institutions, communities, and security actors.

Civic Engagement Programme

Building platforms and tools for citizens' engagement, including youth, women, and underrepresented groups, to effectively participate in governance processes, drive change, and co-create solutions.

Central Africa Programme

Focusing on political, economic, and social developments across the Central African subregion.

East Africa Programme

Analysing and providing independent assessment on political, economic, and social developments across the East African subregion.

North Africa Programme

Driving independent thinking, analysing, and evaluating political, economic, and social developments in the North African region.

Sahel Programme

Monitoring, assessing and evaluating political, economic, and social developments across the Sahel region, including broader regional integration, geopolitical dynamics, political stability, and regional and international relations with a particular focus on the Alliance of Sahel States.

Southern Africa Programme

Focusing on political, economic, and social developments across the Southern African subregion.

West Africa Programme

Monitoring and evaluating political, economic, and social developments across the West African subregion, including regional integration, governance, and democratic stability.

International Affairs Programme

Providing independent thinking and conducting evidence-based policy research and analysis on how international regimes, geopolitics, and global events impact Africa, providing policy advice and recommendations on how African states and institutions could effectively promote national and regional interests and development in a competing and divided world system.

Nigeria in Africa and the World Programme

Serves as a hub for strategic, long-term, independent thinking, analysis, and research about Nigeria's governance and development realities, foreign policy choices, and relationships with Africa and the world.

Regional Security Programme

Monitor regional dynamics and provide cutting-edge, policy-relevant research on the regional security landscape, focusing on the root causes and impact of non-state violence.

Regional Institutions and Governance Programme

Providing evidence-based research and policy analyses on the role and policies of regional institutions in Africa and how regional actors, such as the African Union (AU), Economic Community of West African States (ECOWAS, Southern African Development Community (SADC), East African Community (EAC), Economic Community of Central African States (ECCAS), Arab Maghreb Union (AMU), and others, can strengthen regional governance, fostering greater integration and stability, and promote regional peace, security, prosperity, and development in Africa.
